# Coffee-shop-Bright-sales-analysis
Conduct sales analysis for CEO to understand the performance of the shop in last 6 months.

### Problem Statement
The Coffee shop has been underperforming in the last 6 months and I am tasked to perform a dignotics on reason for drop in revenue.

## The aim of this project is to give analysis with important insights on revenue decline

## Objectives
The following steps were followed to execute the project.
-Project planning using Canva.
-Brainstorm Ideas using Miro.
-Data processing using Data bricks.
- Excel data have been processed to gain insight and create graphs and charts to depict more information on reasons for low performance.
- Presentation has been compiled to show more insight on revenue flactuations across the 6 months period with a particular focus on product category, type and store location amongst others.

## Summary
The Coffee shop has generated over R698 812.33 SALES over the past 6 month.
The Hell's Kitchen store has been the highest performing store over the period.
on our persentation we have included more insights pertaining to the sales decline and how best to improve the figures across the stores.
Recommenadations and implementations have been sugggested on our presentation to aid in improving revenue.
More focus needs to be put on Lower Manhattan store as it is the lowest performing store.
I have recommended we increase social media presence in order to booste sales and attract a new younger market.
